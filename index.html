About Me

Graduate in B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning from M S Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences, and I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Diagnostics, Data, and Digital Health at the University of Warwick. During my academic journey, I have been an active IEEE Student Member, presenting my research at conferences and gaining hands-on industry experience through internships at Dassault Systèmes and L&T Technology Services.

At Dassault Systèmes, I worked on the 3DEXPERIENCE platform, developing solutions for CSI partner Tech Mahindra, while at L&T Technology Services, I contributed to projects involving machine learning, deep learning models, and real-world data analysis for manufacturing processes. My key projects include “Smart Non-Invasive Health Monitoring: Real-Time Insights using ML and IoT” and “Tooth GenAI: Advanced Dental Diagnosis and Treatment Planning System Through AI Integration.”

I am passionate about merging AI with real-world applications, particularly in healthcare, and am eager to leverage my expertise in AI, ML, and digital health to drive innovation and impactful change in the field.

Projects

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I’ve had the privilege of working on innovative projects that reflect my passion for artificial intelligence, machine learning, and their real-world applications. These projects allowed me to solve complex problems, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and explore advanced technologies.

Each project showcases my ability to integrate AI and ML models with modern technologies like IoT, cloud computing, and big data analytics. From healthcare to engineering, these projects illustrate my commitment to using data-driven solutions to address industry-specific challenges.

Smart Non-Invasive Health Monitoring for Glucose

This project focused on building a real-time health monitoring system using wearable IoT devices to gather patient data non-invasively. A key feature was the development of a glucose identification model, which accurately detected blood glucose levels without invasive methods, offering a convenient solution for diabetic patients. Machine learning algorithms processed the data to provide actionable insights, enabling healthcare providers to make more informed decisions, ultimately improving patient outcomes and reducing the strain on traditional healthcare resources.

Tooth GenAI

An AI-powered tool designed to assist dentists in diagnosing dental conditions and planning treatments. The system was deployed at the Faculty of Dental Sciences, offering a data-driven approach to periodontology and improving accuracy in dental assessments. Tooth GenAI highlights the potential of AI in revolutionizing healthcare diagnostics.

Face and Action Detection for Crime Prevention

Developed a novel face and action detection technology for crime prevention and public security. The technology uses real-time capabilities to reduce response times and identify signs of aggression. However, ethical concerns about privacy and potential discrimination need to be addressed. The project aims to use computer vision and data analytics to detect human faces and violent actions in videos. The paper emphasizes the potential of these technologies in ensuring public safety, but calls for cautious and ethical implementation.

IoT Air Pollution on Monitoring System

Developed an advanced IoT-based system for real-time air pollution monitoring, utilizing sensors to collect environmental data. Implemented data analysis and visualization techniques to provide actionable insights on air quality. Enhanced system performance through efficient data processing and integration with cloud services, contributing to improved environmental awareness and public health safety.

Predicting Missing Parts in EBOM

As part of my role at L&T Technology Services, I worked on predicting missing components in the Engineering Bill of Materials (EBOM) for seat assemblies. Using machine learning algorithms, the project significantly improved accuracy in assembly lines, reducing manual intervention and enhancing overall efficiency in production.
